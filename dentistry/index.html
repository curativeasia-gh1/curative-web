3D Facial Scanning

FaceCast MDS — 3D Facial Scanning for Dentistry

FaceCast MDS is Curative's 3D facial scanning platform for dentistry, orthodontics and oral and maxillofacial surgery. It creates a detailed three-dimensional representation of the patient's external facial surface to support facial assessment, multidisciplinary treatment planning and patient communication.

FaceCast MDS · Application

Orthodontic Assessment & Planning

FaceCast MDS supports orthodontic facial analysis by providing three-dimensional records of facial symmetry, profile, proportion and soft-tissue relationships. Facial data can be integrated with intraoral scans and CBCT data for comprehensive orthodontic planning.

Applications: Orthodontics · Clear aligners · Orthognathic planning

FaceCast MDS · Application

Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery

FaceCast MDS provides preoperative facial documentation and postoperative outcome records for oral and maxillofacial surgery cases. Combined with CBCT data, facial scan information can support orthognathic planning and facial asymmetry evaluation.

Applications: OMFS · Orthognathic surgery · Facial asymmetry

FaceCast MDS · Application

Smile Design & Prosthodontics

Adding facial context to digital prosthodontic planning helps clinicians evaluate tooth shape, size, position and smile design in relation to the patient's full facial appearance. FaceCast MDS supports integration with intraoral scan and CBCT data within compatible workflows.

Applications: Smile design · Prosthodontics · Full-mouth rehabilitation

Intraoral Scanning

Digital Impressions for Modern Dental Workflows

Curative Dentistry provides intraoral scanning solutions for fast, accurate and comfortable digital impressions. Intraoral scan data can support restorative dentistry, implantology, orthodontics, prosthodontics and dental laboratory workflows.

RAY · Intraoral Scanner

RAYiOS 2 Intraoral Scanner

RAYiOS 2 is a high-performance intraoral scanner developed by RAY for digital dentistry. It captures high-definition, true-colour digital impressions that help clinicians visualise preparation margins, soft-tissue boundaries and occlusal surfaces. The ergonomic handpiece supports comfortable scanning for both clinician and patient. RAYiOS 2 supports an open digital workflow for compatibility with dental laboratories, design software and CAD/CAM systems. It integrates with RAYFace, compatible CBCT systems and RAYDENT Designer.

Available: Selected Asian markets

Dental CBCT & Imaging

3D Dental Imaging for Diagnosis and Treatment Planning

Curative Dentistry provides dental cone beam computed tomography (CBCT) systems for three-dimensional diagnostic imaging supporting implant planning, orthodontics, oral surgery, endodontics and maxillofacial applications.

Genoray · Dental CBCT

Genoray GT300 Dental CBCT System

The Genoray GT300 is a dental cone beam CT system providing 3D imaging for implant planning, orthodontic assessment, oral surgery, endodontic evaluation, airway analysis, TMJ assessment and maxillofacial applications. The system supports configurable fields of view, high-resolution imaging and integration with compatible panoramic and cephalometric imaging options within digital dental workflows.

Available: Selected Asian markets

Genoray · CBCT Applications

CBCT Clinical Applications

Dental CBCT imaging through Curative supports a broad range of clinical workflows including implant site assessment, bone quantity and quality evaluation, nerve canal visualisation, impacted tooth assessment, orthodontic skeletal analysis, orthognathic surgical planning, airway and temporomandibular joint evaluation, and endodontic root canal anatomy.

Note: CBCT examinations must be clinically justified and performed using an appropriate exposure protocol.
