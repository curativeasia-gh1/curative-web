About SkinClinic

Clinical practice, expertise and visible skincare results.

SkinClinic is a Spanish professional dermocosmetic brand with more than three decades of experience developing high-performance skincare for aesthetic doctors, dermatologists and qualified skincare professionals. Its portfolio combines professional cabin products with home-care solutions, enabling clinics to build coherent programmes around each patient's primary skin concern.

Professional Heritage

Built for medical-dispensing and professional skincare environments. Formulated to the standards expected by dermatologists and aesthetic clinicians.

Research & Formulation

Developed by an in-house R&D team using both established active ingredients and next-generation dermocosmetic technology.

Concern-Led Programmes

Products organised around practical skin objectives — pigmentation, acne, ageing, sensitivity and recovery — rather than cosmetic trends.

Clinic-to-Home Continuity

Professional cabin treatments supported by daily maintenance and recovery routines, creating a coherent patient journey from clinic to home.

Relevance to Asian Markets

Designed around the concerns most relevant to Asian skin.

Year-round ultraviolet exposure, heat, humidity, air-conditioning, urban pollution and frequent aesthetic procedures create distinct skincare needs. SkinClinic gives professionals a structured portfolio for the concerns most commonly seen across Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand and Hong Kong.

Professional System

From professional treatment to daily home care.

SkinClinic connects clinic treatments with practical home-care recommendations, supporting a more coherent patient journey.

1

Assess

A qualified professional identifies the main concern, skin type and individual tolerance. Product and protocol selection follows clinical assessment.

2

Treat

Selected professional cabin products and in-clinic protocols support the treatment objective, from peels and depigmenting procedures to recovery treatments.

3

Maintain

A structured home-care routine supports continuity, barrier recovery and long-term management, including concern-specific daily and night care.

Evening

Evening
