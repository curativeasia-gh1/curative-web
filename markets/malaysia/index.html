Malaysia healthcare market

A growing public and private healthcare ecosystem

Malaysia combines a broad public healthcare network with an established private hospital and specialist-clinic sector. Kuala Lumpur and Selangor are the largest commercial centres, while Johor Bahru and Penang are important regional markets. Demand for specialist medical technology is supported by an ageing population, private healthcare investment, medical tourism and increasing adoption of digital clinical workflows.
