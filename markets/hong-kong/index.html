Market Overview

Two connected healthcare markets in the Greater Bay Area.

Singapore is a high-income, internationally connected economy with an advanced healthcare system and strong regulatory standards. Its total population reached approximately 6.11 million in June 2025, supported by a diverse resident and international community.

The country serves as an important regional centre for healthcare, biomedical sciences, clinical education and medical technology. Singapore's ageing population and continued expansion of healthcare capacity are driving demand for specialist care, efficient clinical workflows and innovative healthcare solutions.

Healthcare Sectors

Three sectors. One specialist partner.

Curative operates across ophthalmology, medical aesthetics and digital dentistry in Hong Kong and Macau, serving hospitals, specialist clinics and private practices across both markets.

Medical Devices

Ophthalmology

Hong Kong has a well-developed ophthalmology market supported by public hospitals, private hospitals, specialist eye centres and experienced ophthalmic surgeons. Demand is driven by cataract surgery, retinal care, glaucoma management and advanced diagnostic services.

Key opportunity areas include intraocular lenses, cataract and vitreoretinal surgical systems, surgical instruments, viscoelastic products and consumables. Adoption is influenced by surgeon preference, clinical evidence and dependable local support. Macau provides a smaller extension market through private ophthalmology and cataract care.

Intraocular Lenses Cataract Surgery Vitreoretinal OVDs Surgical Instruments
Medical Aesthetics

Aesthetics & Dermocosmetics

Hong Kong has a mature and highly competitive medical-aesthetics market with strong demand for injectable treatments, skin-quality improvement, regenerative aesthetics and professional skincare. The market is influenced by experienced consumers, physician reputation and established international brands.

Macau provides a smaller but attractive extension market. Its compact clinic network, tourism economy and high-spending customer base can support premium aesthetic treatments and professional skincare, particularly when coordinated with Hong Kong.

Professional Skincare 3D Facial Imaging Pigmentation Anti-Ageing Injectable Support
Dentistry & Digital Imaging

Digital Dentistry

Hong Kong has an established dental sector comprising private practices, specialist centres, dental groups and public services. Digital adoption is increasing across intraoral scanning, CBCT imaging, facial scanning, implant planning and CAD/CAM workflows. Macau's dental market is smaller and more concentrated, with opportunities for technologies that improve clinical efficiency and higher-value restorative workflows.

Intraoral Scanning CBCT Imaging Facial Scanning CAD/CAM Digital Implant
Curative in Hong Kong & Macau

Our principal base for the Greater Bay Area.

Hong Kong serves as Curative Group's principal base for the Hong Kong and Macau markets and an important platform for regional business development.
