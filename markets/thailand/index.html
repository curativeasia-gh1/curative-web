Market Overview

A focused healthcare market for eye care and professional skincare.

Thailand is one of Southeast Asia’s important healthcare markets, supported by an established public healthcare system, a strong private hospital sector and a well-developed medical tourism industry. Curative Thailand focuses its local strategy on ophthalmology and SkinClinic professional skincare, where clinical education, reliable supply and specialist support are essential.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Curative in Thailand.

What healthcare sectors does Curative serve in Thailand?

Curative Thailand focuses on ophthalmology and SkinClinic professional skincare, supporting hospitals, eye centres, dermatologists, aesthetic physicians and specialist clinics.

What regulatory framework applies to medical devices in Thailand?

Medical devices in Thailand are regulated by the Thai Food and Drug Administration. Products must follow applicable classification, registration, licensing and post-market requirements before commercial supply.

Which cities does Curative focus on in Thailand?

Curative focuses primarily on Bangkok as the main healthcare and commercial centre, with additional opportunities in Chiang Mai, Phuket, Chonburi and other key regional cities.

How can Thailand clinics arrange a product demonstration?

Healthcare professionals and clinics in Thailand can contact Curative directly by email or through the website to arrange a product demonstration, SkinClinic training or clinical evaluation.
