Market Overview

A major healthcare and medical tourism market in Southeast Asia.

Thailand is one of Southeast Asia's largest healthcare markets, supported by an established public healthcare system, a strong private hospital sector and a well-developed medical tourism industry.

Thailand's population is projected to reach approximately 67.1 million in 2026. Its ageing population, large domestic patient base and concentration of specialist hospitals in major cities continue to drive demand for medical devices, ophthalmology and medical-aesthetic solutions.

Bangkok and its surrounding provinces form the country's largest healthcare market. Chiang Mai serves as the main hub for Northern Thailand, while Phuket, Chonburi and southern tourism destinations provide additional opportunities in private healthcare, aesthetics and international patient services.

Healthcare Sectors

Curative operates across ophthalmology, medical aesthetics and specialist medical devices in Thailand, supporting public hospitals, private hospital groups and specialist clinics across Bangkok and key regional centres.

Medical Devices

Ophthalmology

Thailand has a well-developed ophthalmology sector supported by university hospitals, government institutions, private hospitals and specialist eye centres. Demand is driven by cataract surgery, retinal disease, glaucoma, refractive care and age-related eye conditions.

Key opportunity areas include intraocular lenses, cataract and vitreoretinal surgical systems, ophthalmic diagnostics, surgical instruments and procedure-related consumables. Successful market development depends on regulatory approval, surgeon evaluation, clinical education and consistent local support.

Intraocular Lenses Cataract Surgery Vitreoretinal OVDs Surgical Instruments
Medical Aesthetics

Aesthetics & Dermocosmetics

Thailand is one of Asia's most active medical-aesthetics markets, supported by a large clinic network, strong consumer awareness, medical tourism and broad acceptance of minimally invasive treatments.

Demand includes skin-quality treatments, regenerative aesthetics, injectables, pigmentation management and professional skincare. Bangkok is the principal market, with additional opportunities in Chiang Mai, Phuket, Pattaya and other major cities and tourism destinations. The market is highly competitive and requires credible clinical positioning and sustained local support.

Professional Skincare 3D Facial Imaging Pigmentation Anti-Ageing Injectable Support
Dentistry & Digital Imaging

Digital Dentistry

Thailand has a broad network of public and private dental providers, including government hospitals, university dental schools, independent practices and expanding dental groups. Digital adoption is growing, particularly in private clinics in Bangkok, Chiang Mai and medical tourism destinations. Key areas include intraoral scanning, CBCT imaging, implant planning and CAD/CAM workflows.

Intraoral Scanning CBCT Imaging Facial Scanning CAD/CAM Digital Implant
