Curative Markets

Curative operates directly in Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Hong Kong and Macau. We support hospitals, specialist clinics, healthcare professionals and manufacturers through local distribution, market development, training and customer support.

Our market structure is simple: five principal direct markets—Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Hong Kong and Macau. Selected activities elsewhere in Asia may be managed through local entities, distributors or commercial partners. Product availability and distribution rights vary by country.

Frequently asked questions

Which countries are Curative's principal direct markets?

Curative's principal direct markets are Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Hong Kong and Macau.

Does Curative operate in other Asian countries?

Selected activities may be supported through local entities, distributors or commercial partners. These should not be described as full direct markets unless confirmed by current Curative materials.

Are all products available in all five markets?

No. Product registration, distribution rights, commercial availability and approved claims differ by country. Customers should confirm current local status with Curative.
