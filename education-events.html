Education & Events

Clinical Training, Education and Professional Development

Curative supports healthcare professionals across Asia with structured clinical training, product education, application workshops and professional development programmes across ophthalmology, medical aesthetics and digital dentistry.

Clinical Training

Product Training and Application Support

Curative provides structured product training for surgeons, clinicians, clinic staff, operating-room teams and dental professionals across all three divisions and all operating markets.

Ophthalmology Training

IOL selection guidance, injector technique, OVD application, surgical system training, instrument set configuration and operating-room workflow optimisation for cataract and vitreoretinal teams.

  • ·IOL and injector technique
  • ·Surgical system user training
  • ·Instrument set configuration
  • ·Vitreoretinal product training
  • ·Clinical evaluation support

Aesthetics Training

SkinClinic product training and protocol development, FaceCast MPS system training, TSK injection accessory education and skin booster application guidance for aesthetic practitioners.

  • ·SkinClinic range and protocol training
  • ·Clinic onboarding and staff training
  • ·FaceCast MPS system operation
  • ·Home-care programme development
  • ·Treatment protocol guidance

Dentistry Training

Digital workflow training, intraoral scanner onboarding, CBCT imaging guidance, FaceCast MDS operation, CAD/CAM user training and digital dentistry workflow implementation for dental professionals.

  • ·Intraoral scanner technique
  • ·CBCT operation and interpretation
  • ·FaceCast MDS workflow integration
  • ·CAD/CAM and milling user training
  • ·Digital workflow implementation
Clinical Partnerships

KOL Development and Clinical Collaboration

Curative works with key opinion leaders, teaching hospitals and specialist institutions across Asia to develop clinical education, build clinical evidence and support the adoption of innovative healthcare technologies.

KOL Development

Identifying and supporting key opinion leaders who can contribute to clinical education and regional awareness of new technologies.

Clinical Evaluations

Structured product evaluations at leading clinical facilities, with Curative providing full product and application support.

Reference Centres

Developing reference centres and centres of excellence for advanced clinical procedures across each division.

Advisory Collaboration

Working with clinical advisors to ensure product portfolios are appropriate, clinically relevant and well-supported.

Events

Conferences, Workshops and Product Events

Curative participates in and organises clinical education events across Asia throughout the year. Contact us to find out about upcoming events in your market.

Conferences & Scientific Meetings

Curative participates in regional and national ophthalmology, aesthetics and dental conferences as an exhibitor and sponsor. Find us at key meetings across Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand and Hong Kong.

Hands-On Workshops

Webinars & Online Education

Online product education, clinical case presentations and workflow demonstrations for healthcare professionals who prefer digital learning formats.
