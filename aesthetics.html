SkinClinic — Clinical Skincare from Spain for Asian Practices

SkinClinic is a professional clinical skincare brand developed and manufactured in Spain. Its portfolio is formulated for use by dermatologists, aesthetic physicians and qualified skincare professionals, addressing common skin concerns seen in Asian climates — from year-round UV exposure and humidity to pollution-related pigmentation and post-procedure recovery.

Curative is the exclusive regional distributor for SkinClinic across Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand and Hong Kong. The range covers pigmentation care, rosacea and sensitive-skin support, anti-ageing, hydration, acne and sebum management, and post-procedure recovery.

FaceCast MPS · 3D Facial Scanning

FaceCast MPS — 3D Facial Scanner for Aesthetic Medicine

FaceCast MPS is a professional 3D facial scanning system designed for aesthetic medicine, plastic surgery and multidisciplinary clinical applications. It captures a detailed three-dimensional representation of the patient's external facial surface to support clinical assessment, consultation and treatment documentation. Available through Curative in Singapore, Malaysia and Hong Kong.

01

Patient Consultation & Facial Analysis

Three-dimensional facial data supports clinical assessment of facial symmetry, volume, contour and proportion. Clinicians can present visual information during consultation to facilitate more informed treatment discussions with patients.

02

Before-and-After Documentation

Standardised 3D facial records support structured before-and-after documentation across treatment cycles. Consistent 3D capture can improve the clinical value of outcome records compared with conventional photography alone.

03

Multidisciplinary Workflow Support

FaceCast MPS supports collaboration across aesthetic physicians, dermatologists, plastic surgeons and ENT specialists. A shared 3D facial record provides a common visual reference for multidisciplinary treatment planning.

Plinest® · Italian PN HPT® Polynucleotide Treatments · New to Hong Kong

Plinest® PN HPT® — Bio-Regenerative Aesthetic Medicine from Italy

Plinest® is a portfolio of Polynucleotide High Purification Technology (PN HPT®) treatments developed and manufactured by Mastelli in Sanremo, Italy — a pharmaceutical and aesthetic medicine company with over 70 years of heritage. Plinest® is not a conventional dermal filler. It is a bio-regenerative treatment designed to improve skin quality, hydration, texture, elasticity and radiance progressively from within.
