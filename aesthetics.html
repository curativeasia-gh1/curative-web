FaceCast · 3D Facial Scanning

FaceCast MPS — 3D Facial Scanner for Aesthetic Medicine

FaceCast MPS is a professional 3D facial scanning system designed for aesthetic medicine, plastic surgery and multidisciplinary clinical applications. It creates a detailed three-dimensional representation of the patient's external facial surface for clinical assessment, consultation and treatment documentation.

FaceCast · Key Application

Patient Consultation & Facial Analysis

Three-dimensional facial data supports clinical assessment of facial symmetry, volume, contour and proportion. Clinicians can present visual information during consultation to facilitate more informed treatment discussions.

Aesthetic medicine · Plastic surgery

FaceCast · Key Application

Before-and-After Documentation

Standardised 3D facial records support structured before-and-after documentation across treatment cycles. Consistent 3D capture can improve the clinical value of outcome records compared with conventional photography alone.

All aesthetic treatment modalities

FaceCast · Key Application

Multidisciplinary Workflow Support

FaceCast MPS supports collaboration across aesthetic physicians, dermatologists, plastic surgeons and ENT specialists. A shared 3D facial record provides a common visual reference for multidisciplinary treatment planning.

Singapore · Malaysia · Hong Kong

FaceCast is a communication and assessment tool. Any simulation or visualisation should not be presented as a guaranteed treatment outcome. FaceCast is intended for professional clinical use and should not replace physical examination, diagnostic imaging or the clinical judgement of a qualified healthcare professional.

Plinest® · Italian PN HPT® Polynucleotide Treatments

Plinest® Polynucleotides PN HPT®

Curative is proud to introduce the Plinest® PN HPT® aesthetic medicine portfolio to the region — Italian regenerative treatments designed to support skin quality, hydration, texture, elasticity and radiance from within the skin.

Developed and manufactured in Sanremo, Italy since 1952 by Mastelli, Polynucleotides High Purification Technology (PN HPT®) represents a regenerative approach to aesthetic medicine — not a conventional dermal filler, but a bio-regenerative treatment designed to improve the condition and appearance of the skin progressively and naturally.

The primary Plinest® launch portfolio available through Curative comprises three distinct products: Plinest®, Plinest® Eye and Newest® — each addressing a different patient profile, skin area and treatment objective.
