SkinClinic Professional Dermocosmetics

SkinClinic is a professional clinical skincare brand developed and manufactured in Spain. Its portfolio is designed for use by dermatologists, aesthetic physicians and qualified skincare professionals, addressing common skin concerns seen in Asian climates including pigmentation, acne, ageing, sensitivity, rosacea and skin recovery. Available exclusively through Curative in Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand and Hong Kong.

Melanyc — Pigmentation Care

A targeted pigmentation range addressing dark spots, uneven tone and visible signs of sun exposure. Particularly relevant in Asian markets with year-round UV exposure. May complement in-clinic pigmentation treatments when recommended by a practitioner.

Rosanic — Sensitive & Redness-Prone Skin

A calming, barrier-focused range for skin prone to redness, discomfort and visible reactivity. Suitable for patients requiring gentle daily care alongside professional management of redness-prone skin conditions.

Hydro-Nourishing — Intensive Hydration

Intensive hydration and nourishment for dry, dehydrated or compromised skin. Particularly relevant in climates with combined outdoor heat and prolonged indoor air-conditioning exposure.

Acne & Sebum Regulation

Targeted solutions for oily, congested and acne-prone skin. Designed to support cleansing, oil control, skin renewal and maintenance of a balanced complexion within a professionally guided programme.

Anti-Ageing & Luminosity

Formulations intended to support the appearance of smoother, more radiant skin. May be used as part of a daily maintenance programme or alongside professionally administered aesthetic treatments.

Skin Recovery & Post-Procedure Care

Regeneration and recovery products for incorporation into professional aftercare programmes following selected aesthetic procedures, supporting hydration, barrier recovery and skin comfort.

FaceCast · 3D Facial Scanning

FaceCast MPS — 3D Facial Scanner for Aesthetic Medicine

FaceCast MPS is a professional 3D facial scanning system designed for aesthetic medicine, plastic surgery and multidisciplinary clinical applications. It creates a detailed three-dimensional representation of the patient's external facial surface for clinical assessment, consultation and treatment documentation.

FaceCast · Key Application

Patient Consultation & Facial Analysis

Three-dimensional facial data supports clinical assessment of facial symmetry, volume, contour and proportion. Clinicians can present visual information during consultation to facilitate more informed treatment discussions.

FaceCast MPS: Aesthetic medicine · Plastic surgery

FaceCast · Key Application

Before-and-After Documentation

Standardised 3D facial records support structured before-and-after documentation across treatment cycles. Consistent 3D capture can improve the clinical value of outcome records compared with conventional photography alone.

Applications: All aesthetic treatment modalities

FaceCast · Key Application

Multidisciplinary Workflow Support

FaceCast MPS supports collaboration across aesthetic physicians, dermatologists, plastic surgeons and ENT specialists. A shared 3D facial record provides a common visual reference for multidisciplinary treatment planning and communication.

Available: Singapore · Malaysia · Hong Kong

FaceCast is a communication and assessment tool. Any simulation or visualisation should not be presented as a guaranteed treatment outcome. FaceCast is intended for professional clinical use and should not replace physical examination, diagnostic imaging or the clinical judgement of a qualified healthcare professional.
