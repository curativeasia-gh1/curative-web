Intraocular Lenses

IOL Solutions for Every Patient

Curative provides a comprehensive range of intraocular lens technologies covering monofocal, toric and trifocal options for personalised vision correction after cataract surgery.

HOYA Surgical Optics · Monofocal IOL

Vivinex™ iSert® (XC1 / XY1)

The Vivinex™ iSert is a hydrophobic acrylic monofocal IOL delivered via the preloaded iSert® injector system. The aspheric optic design features a square posterior edge and active oxygen processing treatment. Available in UV-filter (XC1) and UV- and blue-light-filter (XY1) options. Power range +6.00 to +30.00 D in 0.50 D increments. Recommended incision size 2.20 mm.

Available: Singapore (authorised distributor)

Request Information

HOYA Surgical Optics · Toric IOL

Vivinex™ Toric multiSert® (XY1A-SP)

The Vivinex™ Toric multiSert combines astigmatism correction with the proven Vivinex hydrophobic acrylic platform. Delivered through the multiSert® preloaded injector for controlled, consistent implantation. Designed for rotational stability to support reliable astigmatic correction following cataract surgery.

Available: Singapore (authorised distributor)

Request Information

HOYA Surgical Optics · Trifocal IOL

Vivinex™ Gemetric™ Trifocal Family

The Gemetric™ trifocal IOL family provides a broad range of vision across distance, intermediate and near. Gemetric™ (XY1-G) prioritises distance with balanced intermediate and near. Gemetric™ Plus (XY1-GP) prioritises near with balanced distance and intermediate. Toric configurations (XY1-GT, XY1-GPT) are available for patients with corneal astigmatism. Delivered via the multiSert® preloaded injector.

Available: Singapore (authorised distributor)

Request Information

VOPTICA · Premium IOL Platform

ArtIOL® 25 / ArtIOL® 40 / ArtIOL® 70

The VOPTICA ArtIOL® family features a patented inverted meniscus optical design inspired by the natural crystalline lens. Unlike conventional biconvex IOLs, the inverted meniscus geometry is designed to enhance peripheral and off-axis optical performance. ArtIOL® 25, 40 and 70 offer different levels of non-diffractive depth of focus, allowing surgeons to personalise lens selection according to each patient's visual priorities and lifestyle.

Available: Hong Kong · Malaysia · Thailand

Cataract Surgery

Systems and Accessories for Phacoemulsification

From phacoemulsification platforms and IOL injectors to OVDs and custom procedure kits, Curative supports the complete cataract surgical workflow.

Oertli · Cataract Surgical System

CataRhex 3® Phacoemulsification System

The Oertli CataRhex 3® is a compact phacoemulsification system developed in Switzerland for routine cataract surgery. Featuring Oertli's controlled fluidics technology, the system supports precise aspiration and irrigation control during anterior segment procedures. Suitable for high-volume cataract programmes in hospital and ambulatory surgical settings.

Available: Hong Kong

OPHTEC · IOL Injector System

RingJect™ Injector System

The OPHTEC RingJect™ is an IOL injector system designed to support controlled and consistent intraocular lens implantation. It is compatible with selected IOL platforms and is designed to minimise direct handling of the lens during implantation, supporting operating-room efficiency and safety.

ODC · IOL Injector System

ErgoTouch GLIDE Injector

The ODC ErgoTouch GLIDE is a preloaded IOL injector system designed for ergonomic, single-handed operation during cataract surgery. Its design supports smooth and controlled lens delivery to reduce force and improve implantation consistency. Available for use with compatible IOL platforms.

Ariavisc · Ophthalmic Viscoelastic Device

Ariavisc OVD (Sodium Hyaluronate)

Ariavisc ophthalmic viscoelastic devices are sodium hyaluronate-based OVDs available in dispersive and cohesive formulations for cataract and anterior-segment surgery. Designed to support anterior-chamber maintenance, corneal endothelial protection, capsular-bag expansion and controlled IOL implantation.

Vitreoretinal Surgery

Posterior Segment Surgical Solutions

From advanced vitreoretinal surgical platforms to specialty single-use instruments and retinal tamponades, Curative supports posterior-segment surgical teams in selected Asian markets.

Oertli · Vitreoretinal Surgical System

Faros™ Phaco & Vitreoretinal System

The Oertli Faros™ is a versatile surgical platform supporting phacoemulsification and vitreoretinal procedures. Available configurations include phaco, controlled fluidics, vitrectomy, illumination, diathermy and integrated endolaser capabilities. Suitable for combined anterior- and posterior-segment procedures in specialist eye centres and hospitals.

Available: Hong Kong

Oertli · Advanced Vitreoretinal System

OS 4™ Up Vitreoretinal System

The Oertli OS 4™ Up is an advanced vitreoretinal surgical platform for complex posterior-segment procedures. The system supports high-speed vitrectomy, controlled fluidics and a range of surgical configurations for demanding vitreoretinal cases, including proliferative vitreoretinopathy, giant retinal tears and retinal detachment surgery.

Available: Hong Kong

ACTU8® Retinal Forceps

Vortex Surgical ACTU8® retinal forceps are specialised single-use instruments designed for delicate posterior-segment tissue manipulation. MedOne Surgical · Single-Use Retina Instruments

MedOne Surgical Retina Portfolio

MedOne Surgical provides single-use cannulas, backflush instruments and fluid-delivery devices for modern small-gauge vitreoretinal surgery. Compatible with 20-, 23-, 25- and 27-gauge platforms. Micromed Silicone Oils, Gases & PFCL
