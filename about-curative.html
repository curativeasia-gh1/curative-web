About Curative Group

A Specialist Regional Healthcare Partner Across Asia

Curative Group is a privately owned regional healthcare company headquartered in Singapore. We connect clinically relevant medical technologies with hospitals, specialist clinics and healthcare professionals across Asia through specialist distribution, clinical education, market development and long-term support.

Contact CurativePartner With Us

Our Mission

To bring clinically relevant medical technologies to healthcare professionals across Asia through strong local support, education and long-term partnerships.

Who We Are

More Than a Distributor

Curative Group positions itself as a specialist healthcare commercialisation partner — not a general medical trading company. We combine deep regional market knowledge with clinical credibility, dedicated local teams and long-term commitment to every technology we represent.

Our three core divisions — Ophthalmology, Medical Aesthetics and Dentistry & Digital Imaging — are each supported by product specialists, application trainers and clinical teams with sector-specific expertise.

We work with international healthcare manufacturers to introduce products to Asian markets and with local healthcare professionals to provide the clinical support, training and supply infrastructure needed to use those products effectively.

What We Do

End-to-End Healthcare Commercialisation

Curative provides a comprehensive range of services that takes a technology from initial market assessment through to long-term supply and clinical support — making us a genuine commercialisation partner, not simply a reseller.

Our services are available across all three divisions and can be configured to suit the needs of international manufacturers, local healthcare institutions and individual practitioners.

Market Assessment & Entry

Evaluating market opportunity, competitive landscape, regulatory pathways and commercial strategy for new products entering Asia.

Regulatory Documentation Support

Assisting with regulatory documentation, registration requirements and market-specific approval coordination.

Product Demonstrations & Clinical Evaluation

Arranging hands-on evaluations at clinical facilities, supporting surgeon and practitioner assessment of new products.

Clinical Education & KOL Development

Workshops, CME programmes, case-based training and key opinion leader engagement across all three divisions.

Installation, Training & Technical Support

Installation, commissioning, user training and technical-service coordination for equipment and digital systems.

Inventory & Supply Planning

Collaborative forecasting, inventory management and supply-chain coordination across all markets.

Our Approach

How We Work

Curative combines international healthcare technologies with local implementation — bringing clinical credibility, regional expertise and long-term commitment to every partnership.

01

Clinical First

We select and represent products that are clinically relevant and commercially viable in our markets. We do not distribute everything — we choose carefully.

02

Local Knowledge

Our teams understand the healthcare systems, procurement processes, regulatory environments and clinical cultures in each of our markets.

03

Education-Led

We invest in clinical education, product training and KOL development because adoption depends on knowledge, not just availability.

04

Long-Term Partnership

We commit to the technologies and manufacturers we represent — providing sustained commercial, clinical and technical support over the long term.
