About Curative Group

Curative Group is a Singapore-headquartered healthcare technology and medical device distribution group. We help international manufacturers enter and grow in Asian healthcare markets through specialist distribution, local market development, clinical education, regulatory coordination and long-term customer support.

Curative operates directly in Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Hong Kong and Macau. Selected activities in other Asian markets may be supported through local entities, distributors or commercial partners. Product availability and distribution rights vary by country.

What Curative does

Market entry and distribution

We assess market opportunities, coordinate local commercialisation and build specialist sales channels for healthcare technologies.

Clinical and application support

We support demonstrations, product evaluations, training, reference-site development and adoption by healthcare professionals.

Regional commercial support

We coordinate supply, local customer support, market feedback, regulatory activities and manufacturer relationships across our active markets.

How Curative is different

Curative is positioned as a specialist healthcare commercialisation partner rather than a general trading company. Each division is supported by product knowledge, local market understanding, clinical application support and long-term manufacturer engagement.

Frequently asked questions

Which countries does Curative serve directly?

Curative's principal direct markets are Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Hong Kong and Macau. Activities elsewhere in Asia may be handled selectively through local entities, distributors or commercial partners.

Does every Curative product have the same availability in every country?

No. Product registration, distribution rights, commercial availability and intended use differ by jurisdiction. Customers should confirm current status with Curative before relying on website information.

Does Curative work only with hospitals?

No. Curative works with hospitals, specialist clinics, healthcare professionals, laboratories, distributors and international healthcare manufacturers.
