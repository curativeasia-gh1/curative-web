About Curative Group

A Specialist Regional Healthcare Partner Across Asia

Curative Group is a Singapore-headquartered healthcare technology and medical device distribution group. We connect clinically relevant technologies with hospitals, specialist clinics and healthcare professionals through specialist distribution, clinical education, market development and long-term support.

Contact CurativePartner With Us

Our Mission

To bring clinically relevant medical technologies to healthcare professionals across Asia through strong local support, education and long-term partnerships.

Who We Are

More Than a Distributor

Curative is positioned as a specialist healthcare commercialisation partner rather than a general medical trading company. We combine regional market knowledge with product expertise, local support and long-term commitment to the technologies we represent.

We work with international healthcare manufacturers to introduce products to Asian markets and with local healthcare professionals to provide the training, supply infrastructure and application support needed for effective adoption.

Curative operates directly in Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Hong Kong and Macau. Selected activities elsewhere in Asia may be supported through local entities, distributors or commercial partners. Product availability and distribution rights vary by country.

Frequently Asked Questions

About Curative Group

Which countries does Curative serve directly?

Curative's principal direct markets are Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Hong Kong and Macau. Activities elsewhere in Asia may be handled selectively through local entities, distributors or commercial partners.

Does every Curative product have the same availability in every country?

No. Product registration, distribution rights, commercial availability and intended use differ by jurisdiction. Customers should confirm current status with Curative.

Does Curative work only with hospitals?

No. Curative works with hospitals, specialist clinics, healthcare professionals, laboratories, distributors and international healthcare manufacturers.
