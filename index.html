About Curative Group

Singapore-Based.
Asia-Focused. B2B Only.

Curative Group Pte. Ltd. is incorporated in Singapore and operates exclusively as a B2B distributor to licensed clinics, hospitals, and healthcare institutions across four markets.

We hold authorised distributor status for HOYA Surgical Optics (Singapore) and serve as exclusive ASEAN distributor for SkinClinic Spain, among other principal relationships across Europe, Spain, the USA, and South Korea.
