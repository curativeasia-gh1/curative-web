Featured Solutions

Selected Technologies From Our Portfolio

A representative selection of the clinical technologies and products available through Curative. Availability varies by market.

Ophthalmology · IOLs

HOYA Vivinex™ IOL Family

The Vivinex™ iSert monofocal, multiSert, Toric multiSert and Gemetric™ trifocal IOLs provide a comprehensive preloaded intraocular lens platform for cataract surgery. Developed by HOYA Surgical Optics.

Available in Singapore (authorised distributor)

Ophthalmology · Premium IOLs

VOPTICA ArtIOL® Platform

The ArtIOL® 25, ArtIOL® 40 and ArtIOL® 70 offer a tiered approach to premium intraocular lens correction for cataract and refractive surgery, developed by VOPTICA.

Available in Hong Kong, Malaysia and Thailand

Ophthalmology · Surgical Systems

Oertli Surgical Platforms

The Oertli CataRhex 3®, Faros™ and OS 4™ Up provide Swiss-engineered phacoemulsification and vitreoretinal surgery capabilities for anterior and posterior segment procedures.

Available in Hong Kong

Aesthetics · Dermocosmetics

SkinClinic Professional Dermocosmetics

A clinical skincare range developed in Spain for aesthetic clinics, dermatologists and medical spas. Addresses pigmentation, acne, ageing, sensitivity, rosacea and skin recovery. Exclusive regional distribution through Curative.

Available in Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand and Hong Kong

Aesthetics · Facial Imaging

FaceCast MPS 3D Facial Scanner

A professional 3D facial scanning system designed for aesthetic medicine, plastic surgery and multidisciplinary clinical applications including patient consultation, treatment documentation and before-and-after assessment.

Available in selected markets including Singapore, Malaysia and Hong Kong

Aesthetics · Injection Accessories

TSK Laboratory Aesthetic Range

STERiGLIDE™ cannulas, STERiJECT™ needles and THE INViSIBLE NEEDLE™ provide a comprehensive range of injection accessories for aesthetic practitioners. Available in selected Asian markets.

Available in selected Asian markets

Dentistry · Intraoral Scanning

RAYiOS 2 Intraoral Scanner

A digital intraoral scanner developed by RAY for restorative dentistry, implantology and orthodontic workflows. The RAYiOS 2 supports digital impression acquisition within compatible CAD/CAM and treatment-planning workflows.

Available in selected Asian markets

Dentistry · CBCT Imaging

Genoray GT300 Dental CBCT

A cone beam computed tomography (CBCT) system for 3D dental imaging supporting implant planning, orthodontic assessment, oral surgery and maxillofacial applications within digital dental workflows.

Available in selected Asian markets

Dentistry · Clinic Equipment

Dentis LUVIS Dental Chairs & Lighting

Professional dental chairs and surgical lighting systems developed by Dentis. LUVIS units are designed to support ergonomic clinical environments and optimal illumination for dental and oral surgical procedures.

Available in selected Asian markets

Support Services

Comprehensive Support at Every Stage

Curative provides clinical, technical and commercial support throughout the product lifecycle — from initial evaluation to long-term supply.

Product Demonstrations

Hands-on demonstrations for clinical evaluation, arranged at your facility or through our regional showrooms. Available for surgical systems, digital equipment and skincare ranges.

Clinical Education & Training

Product training, surgical skills workshops, CME and CPD programmes, case-based education and KOL development across ophthalmology, aesthetics and dentistry.

Installation & Technical Support

Installation, commissioning, user training, preventive maintenance and corrective-support coordination for surgical systems, imaging equipment and digital dentistry platforms.

Inventory & Supply Planning

Collaborative inventory management, product forecasting and supply-chain coordination to support consistent product availability across markets.

Regulatory Documentation Support

Marketing & Market Development

Digital marketing, patient education materials, clinic staff training, promotional campaigns and brand-building support in each market territory.
